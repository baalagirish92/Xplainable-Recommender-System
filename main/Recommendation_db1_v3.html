Things to know :

This is a Graph based Research paper Recommendation system which generates recommendations on the following three Sub-graphs :-

  1. Topic Subgraph- Containing Users, papers and associated Topics generated based on the content of the papers
  2. Research Interests and Activities Subgraph- Containing papers, users, their research interests and the activities they were involved in.
  3. Department Subgraph- Containing papers, users and the departments they belong to.
Similarity score between the user profile and paper is calculated in individual sub-graphs and averaged out to generate the top 10 recommendations shown below. These recommendation carry a one-line explanation as to which of the subgraph score was the highest in contributing to the overall score. Below the Top 10 recommendations, are the recommendations based on similarity scores in each of the sub-graphs individually.
Click on the paper title to reveal the paper properties(language, type, abstract etc.) and the path from user to the paper.
